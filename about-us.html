About Us

Helping Stockport residents navigate planning applications with ease

Our Mission

skplanning.org was created to make Stockport planning applications easier to find, search, and understand. We believe that local communities should have clear, accessible access to planning information without needing to navigate complex systems.

What We Do

We provide a simple, independent website that allows residents, businesses, and community groups to:

  • Search and view planning applications across Stockport
  • Track decisions and outcomes on local developments
  • Stay informed about changes in their neighbourhood

Independent Resource

Not Affiliated: We are not connected to Stockport Metropolitan Borough Council. All application data shown on this site is sourced directly from public records and official council data.
